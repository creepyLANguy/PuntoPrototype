Purpose-built scoring hardware.

Pulse

Direct player control with a tiny portable device designed for fast gameplay.

  • Single-button scoring
  • Hold actions for undo, switch teams, and reset
  • Audible score feedback
  • NFC registration support
  • Ideal for casual matches and club rentals

Beacon

Automatic scoring interactions using distance sensing technology.

  • Distance-based score triggering
  • Physical team assignment switch
  • Minimal interaction required
  • Rechargeable battery
  • Ideal for high-traffic and competitive courts

Hub

The connection point for full smart court functionality and club-wide management.

  • NFC reader integration
  • Device-to-court registration
  • Court management tools
  • Smart device allocation
  • Ideal for multi-court venues
