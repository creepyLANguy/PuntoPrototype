Pulse

Direct, portable control.

  • Single-button scoring
  • Undo, switch teams, and reset actions
  • Ideal for casual matches and club rentals

Beacon

Hands-free, intuitive scoring.

  • Ultrasonic sensor
  • Physical team assignment
  • Most natural interaction for players
  • Built for high-traffic, competitive courts

Hub

Full smart court integration.

  • RFID-based triggers
  • Extended battery life
  • Central control for all devices
  • Perfect for venues with multiple courts

Every device includes rechargeable batteries, USB-C power, smart Wi-Fi, audio feedback, and RFID integration.
